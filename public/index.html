The Power of Donating Blood

Learn who can donate and why it is so important to maintain a healthy blood bank.

🩸 Who Can Donate?
  • Individuals between 18 and 65 years old.
  • Must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • In general good health and feeling well.
  • Have not donated in the last 90 days.
❤️ Why Donate?
  • One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Blood cannot be manufactured; it only comes from generous volunteers.
  • Critical for surgeries, trauma patients, and chronic illnesses.
🩺 Health Benefits
  • Stimulates fresh blood cell production.
  • Provides a free mini health check-up (pulse, BP, hemoglobin).
  • Can improve overall cardiovascular health.
